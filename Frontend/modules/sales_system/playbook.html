💡 Objection Handling Tips
①
Acknowledge first. Never dismiss. Say "That's a fair concern — many principals feel the same initially."
②
Ask before answering. "Can I ask what specifically made you feel the pricing is high?" — understand before responding.
③
Use social proof. Reference a similar school or institution that had the same concern and saw results.
④
Reframe to ROI. Convert objections about cost into a discussion about value and return.
⑤
Confirm & move forward. After responding, always confirm: "Does that address your concern?" then advance.
